History Notes

--------------

Treaty of Versailles
A peace document signed at the end of World War I by the Allied Forces (and associated powers), and by Germany in the Hall of Mirrors in the Palace of Versailles on June 28, 1919.
Reparations
A levy (tax) on a defeated country forcing it to pay some of the war costs of the winning countries.
Germany had to pay a whole bunch of money to the Allied Forces (like the United States) as a result of World War I.
